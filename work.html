Landscaping · Website & local presence

Leaf & Stone

The challenge

Leaf & Stone do beautiful landscaping work, but had no proper way to show it. Prospective customers had nowhere to see past projects, no simple way to request a quote, and the business was near-invisible when people searched for landscapers in the area — the work spoke for itself, but only once you’d already found them.

What we built

A fast, mobile-first website designed around their portfolio — a visual gallery of completed gardens and hardscaping, a clear quote-request form that lands enquiries straight in their inbox, and local-SEO foundations so the business turns up when nearby customers search. Built to load quickly on a phone, because that’s where people browse landscaping ideas.
